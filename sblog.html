If you never try, you’ll never know...

My Blog

I remember the day, crystal clear when I read this quote, for the first time “If you never try, you’ll never know” and my perspective on life changed. I was 18, interning with this organization and this quote poped up during a presentation and it got me thinking….. All of us have a desire to fulfill a goal or a dream to achieve but we’re too scared to try. You must want it so bad that it consumes you. Turn your dream into a mission ,be dedicated, be disciplined, make it your priority. Write it down. And manifest it. You owe it to your future self. If not, you’ll always find excuses to not make it happen. You might fail, you might fail again but when you finally succeed it will all be worth it. So just go for it , cause “If you never try ,you’ll never know....”
